WLI Waterfalls

WLI Waterfalls

Wli falls is the highest waterfall in Ghana and the tallest in West Africa.[1] It has a lower and an upper fall. It is 20 km from Hohoe in the Volta Region of Ghana.A walk through the forest of the Agumatsa wildlife sanctuary offers a chance to see a large colony of fruit bats, butterflies, birds, monkeys and baboons.

Coordinates: 7°07′29″N 0°35′31″E
